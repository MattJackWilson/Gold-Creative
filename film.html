Our Process

Whether you're working with film for the very first time, or you're a seasoned professional looking for a new creative direction, our process always begins with a cup of coffee, a pen, and a piece of paper.

Our initial meeting with you will help us to understand exactly what sort of film you're looking to create, who your target audience is and what results you expect to achieve, be it brand awareness, attracting new customers, fundraising, informative, or straightforward sales. From here we can refine the brief, draft a schedule and provide you with a quote within the parameters of your budgetary allowance.
